Health Japanese Fried Rice

John Smith
15 March 2022

Nutrition Information

Calories 219.9 kcal
Protein 12.3g
Carbs 35.2g
Fat 4.7g
Fiber 2.1g

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked brown rice
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation Time

30 minutes

Servings

2 people

Directions

  1. 1

    Heat the sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.

  2. 2

    Add the minced garlic and saut√© for 30 seconds until fragrant.

  3. 3

    Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until tender-crisp.

  4. 4

    Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and pour the beaten eggs into the other side. Scramble until cooked through.

  5. 5

    Add the cooked brown rice to the skillet and break up any clumps. Stir everything together.

  6. 6

    Drizzle with soy sauce and rice vinegar. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. 7

    Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through.

  8. 8

    Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.

Chef's Tips

  • For best results, use cold leftover rice as it fries better and doesn't get mushy.
  • You can add protein like diced chicken, shrimp, or tofu for a more substantial meal.
  • Adjust the seasoning to your preference - add more soy sauce for saltiness or rice vinegar for tanginess.
